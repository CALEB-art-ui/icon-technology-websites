HP EliteBook 820 G3 Review

The HP EliteBook 820 G3 is a lightweight and compact business laptop designed for professionals on the go. With a sleek design, robust performance, and enterprise-level security features, it’s a solid choice for business users.

HP EliteBook 820 G3 Image Slideshow

Key Features

  • Processor: Intel Core i5-6200U (6th Gen)
  • Display: 12.5-inch HD Anti-Glare Display
  • RAM: 8GB DDR4
  • Storage: 256GB SSD
  • Ports: USB 3.0, USB-C, HDMI, Ethernet, and SD card reader
  • Battery Life: Up to 10 hours

Pros

  • Lightweight and portable design
  • Solid build quality
  • Great keyboard for typing
  • Good performance for office tasks

Cons

  • Not suitable for heavy gaming or video editing
  • Display resolution could be higher
  • No dedicated GPU

Final Thoughts

The HP EliteBook 820 G3 is a dependable business laptop with a great mix of performance, portability, and security features. It’s perfect for professionals who need a compact laptop that can handle day-to-day tasks with ease.
